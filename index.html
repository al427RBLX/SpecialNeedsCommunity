About Us

Who We Are

Inspired by past social movements, the Special Needs Community began in 2020 when a few community members decided to organize and make an impact. Pretty quickly, this singular moment evolved into a full-fledged movement. Our Community Action Group challenges the status quo, holding our leaders accountable and fighting for solutions that will transform society.
