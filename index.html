Our goal is to be an impactful community group led by members of the community. We are inspired by the belief that everyday people can shape and mold a society where can achieve their true potential.

About Us

Who We Are

Inspired by social movements of the past, Special Needs Community began in 2020 when a few community members decided to organize and make an impact. Pretty quickly, this singular moment evolved into a full-fledged movement. Our Community Action Group challenges the status quo, holding our leaders accountable and fighting for solutions that will transform society.
